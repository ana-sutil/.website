QUEENS COFFEE

product design

A manual coffee machine that epitomizes elegance, luxury and regality, taken to the modern and minimal, designed to be integrated into a large, minimalist coffee lover´s kitchen, becoming the center of attention in the place where is found. "Queen's Coffee" is not merely a coffee maker, it's a comprehensive coffee experience, a cherished ritual woven into the fabric of your day. It brings the ambiance of a premium coffee shop right into the comfort of your home, making each coffee moment a significant highlight in your daily routine.

with Lília Amaro and Rita Figueiredo
