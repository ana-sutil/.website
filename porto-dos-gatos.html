PORTO DOS GATOS

graphic design

This establishment introduces a fresh and innovative concept. Operating as a cafeteria, it provides patrons with an assortment of daily meals, snacks, coffee, and more. Notably, Porto dos Gatos offers an exceptional feature - the presence of cats within the premises. Customers are afforded the opportunity to interact with these feline companions during their visit. In fact, those inclined can even choose to adopt these cats as part of their experience at the café.
